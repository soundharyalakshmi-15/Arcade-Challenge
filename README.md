Welcome to **CodeQuest (Feb Challenge) – CLI Arcade** – a place where **code becomes play**, and every line you write builds toward something bigger.

Here, you’ll contribute, experiment, and see your arcade game creations come alive… **all in the terminal**.

---

## 🚀 How to Join the Adventure

1. **Fork** this repository
2. Take on the weekly challenge (from issues) and submit a **pull request**
3. Make sure your code passes all tests
4. Earn points, badges, and recognition

Every contribution matters – by the end, you’ll have built something **playable, fun, and uniquely yours**.

---

## 🌟 Who Can Participate

- 1st, 2nd, and 3rd year students
- Anyone ready to code, learn, and have fun
- Python & C++ enthusiasts

---

## ⚡ Ready to Level Up?

Every week brings a new challenge.  
Are you ready to test your skills, unlock achievements, and see your code in action?

**Note**

- You may use AI tools for guidance or debugging.
- Direct copy-paste of solutions without understanding is discouraged (We are good at finding AI contributions 🐧).
- All PRs must include a short explanation of the logic.

**Let the quest begin!** ✨
